## 2. LOGO SYSTEM

**Five allowed signatures (no single "primary" — choose by context):**
1. Horizontal — `aisalon`
2. Stacked — `ai` / `sa` / `lon` (vertical)
3. Horizontal + tagline
4. Stacked + tagline
5. Monogram `ais` — **restricted** to favicons/avatars/small spaces only

**Construction & spacing:**
- Construction grid exists; clear-space unit = height of the "L" in "aisalon"
- No text, image, or mark may enter the L-based protective area
- **Minimum size: 57 PX / 20 MM** (graphic & digital)

## 4. LOGO-ON-BACKGROUND RULES (strict matrix)

| Background | Allowed logo color | Forbidden |
|---|---|---|
| White | **Black only** | White, Red, Cyan, Accent |
| Black | **White only** | Black, Red, Cyan, Accent |
| AIS Cyan | **Black only** | White, Red, Cyan, Accent |
| AIS Red | **Black only** | White, Red, Cyan, Accent |
| Non-brand color | Black or White (contrast-dependent) | Red, Cyan, Accent |
| Image / photograph | Black or White (contrast-dependent) | Red, Cyan, Accent |

**Iron rule:** The logo itself is **only ever black or white** — never red, never cyan, never an accent color.

## 6. THE MEERKAT — MASCOT & CHARACTER

- **Role:** "Main Character" — the figure users worldwide identify with
- **Symbolism:** Connection + Innovation
- **Style:** Low-poly / polygonal / faceted 3D illustration
- **Palette:** Vibrant multi-color (oranges, yellows, blues, purples, reds, teals) — geometric color blocking
- **Cut-off rule:** When integrating the Meerkat with variable back-shapes (polyhedrons), **the Meerkat's hands must always remain outward** to look natural and integrated

---

## 7. POLYHEDRON "SHAPES" SYSTEM

The brand's signature visual container — free-form abstract polyhedrons whose faces are filled with color or imagery.

| Shape type | Fill rule |
|---|---|
| **GLOBAL shapes** | Filled **exclusively** with institutional colors (AIS GRADIENT, AIS CYAN, AIS CYAN #2). Never tied to a specific city. |
| **CHAPTER shapes** | Take colors from the **chapter city's illustration**, BUT **one face must always carry the AIS GRADIENT** for visual consistency. (Tokyo example colors: `#265E73`, `#9CC3C5`, `#E04945` + AIS GRADIENT face) |
| **CITY LINES** | Connecting lines that interact with SHAPES — link cities near the corresponding Chapter via vertices/edges |
| **EVENT COMBO** | Date+city+country combo (e.g. `Tokyo / JAN 01 / JAP`) — adapts from vertical to fully horizontal layout |

## 8. LAYOUT LANGUAGE

- **Section opener pages:** Full-bleed solid color (cyan, pink, magenta, black) + single word centered in opposite color (e.g. "Logotype" / "Colors" / "Typography" / "Meerkat" / "Elements" / "Visual ID")
- **Chapter opener template (p.33):** Pink background + giant black "1.1" + chapter title in white, with a faint geometric wireframe overlay
- **Two-column layouts** dominate: text left / image right, or text left / illustration right
- **Generous white space** — minimalist, never crowded
- **Geometric overlays on photography:** Pink/cyan polygonal wireframes layered over full-bleed photos for energy
- **Headshot framing:** Faces are placed inside an irregular polygon shape with a pink-to-blue AIS GRADIENT accent on one side
- **Tagline placement:** Always BELOW the logo, smaller weight, uppercase

## 11. TEL AVIV CHAPTER — IMPLICATIONS FOR MARKETING

When building Tel Aviv chapter assets, follow these rules:

1. **Chapter shape colors:** Pull from a Tel Aviv city illustration (e.g. Mediterranean blue, Bauhaus white, Jaffa stone, Bursa green, beach gold) — BUT one face must always carry the **AIS GRADIENT**.
2. **Event combos:** `Tel Aviv / [MONTH] [DAY] / ISR` — flexible vertical/horizontal layouts allowed.
3. **Logo:** Always black on light backgrounds, white on dark — never red, cyan, or accent.
4. **Meerkat:** Hands outward when paired with Tel Aviv polyhedron. Cannot be re-colored; mascot stays in its multi-color form.
5. **Typography:** Plus Jakarta Sans for all marketing assets (web, social, print). Inter as web fallback only.
6. **HEX first:** Always spec colors via HEX values in code/design tools; CMYK/Pantone only for specialty print.
7. **Tagline:** "EMPOWERING AI CONNECTIONS" — uppercase, smaller weight, always below logo.
8. **Speaker cards / event promos:** Use the gradient-polygon headshot frame, "MEET OUR SPEAKER" structure, location pin, stacked logo + tagline.
9. **Roll-up banners / key visuals:** Three allowed patterns — pure Meerkat / Meerkat + chapter polyhedron with city image / photograph with geometric overlay.
10. **Social media icons** (per p.40): LinkedIn, Instagram, X, YouTube, Facebook, TikTok — minimalist white square icons.
